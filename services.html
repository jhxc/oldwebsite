  • Prosthetic arm

    A low cost ($20) prototype prosthesis constructed from surfboard grade, shock and water resistant polystyrene. A microcontroller allows for actuation of each individual finger through 5 servo motors.

    Arduino, C

  • Racing simulator

    A full sized racing simulator constructed from a Formula SAE racecar chassis that was developed for Melbourne University Racing driver training. In addition to this, it is often used in school engagement programs and open days to encourage high school students to gain an interest in engineering.

  • Social media management bot

    Command line tool that automates uploads, follows and likes on Instagram. The bot can be set to fetch images from reddit and upload them to Instagram captioned with the original poster's username and caption. It was paired with a football themed account during the 2018 world cup.

    Uses instapy-CLI and praw to connect to Instagram and reddit.

    Python
